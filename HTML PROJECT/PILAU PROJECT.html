RECIPE

HOW TO COOK BEEF PILAU

Pilau project

INGRIDIENTS

  • 250g beef
  • 2 onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 5cm ginger
  • 2 small bay leaves
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup of sunflower oil
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 2 cups rice

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Peel the onion and garlic cloves.
  2. Wash the beef and add to a pan.Slice in 1 onion and 2 garlic with the ginger. Add a bay leaves and a cup of water and a salt to taste.
  3. Cook the beef until is tender,then remove it from the heat.
  4. Chop the remaining onion and the garlic.
  5. Wash the rice until the water runs clear.
  6. In another pan heat the oil in low heat.
  7. Add the onion in the pot and stir until it turns brown.
  8. One should stir the onion with a wooden spoon to prevent burning.
  9. pour in the garlic and stir it for 30 sec.
  10. Add the rice into the pot.Pour the beef and add water so thier is enough to cook the rice.
  11. Cover then allow the rice to cook until all the moisture is absorbed.
  12. Remove the rice on the heat.

SERVEING

Serve it with a mango juice
